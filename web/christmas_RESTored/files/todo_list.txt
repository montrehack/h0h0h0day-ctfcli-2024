
What to Do When Santa Visits:

1. Leave cookies and milk by the fireplace.
2. Hang stockings by the chimney.
3. Prepare a carrot for the reindeer.
4. Leave a note thanking Santa for the gifts.
5. Ensure the house is cozy and welcoming.
6. Set up a small Christmas tree with lights and decorations.
7. Prepare a special space for Santa to enter (usually via the chimney).
8. Make sure the kids are asleep to avoid disturbing Santa's work.
