
Santa's Christmas Eve Schedule

- 6:00 PM - Prepare sleigh and reindeer for the journey.
- 7:00 PM - Begin delivering gifts in Europe.
- 9:00 PM - Continue gift delivery in Asia.
- 11:00 PM - Arrive in the Americas.
- 2:00 AM - Finish deliveries and head back to the North Pole.
- 4:00 AM - Return to the North Pole to rest for next year's preparations.
